The Hi Spot Cafe

Breakfast Menu | Midday Menu

Our Story:

Originally a lovely Victorian home, the Hi Spot house has been transformed into a hip restaurant. From green eggs n ham to massive cinnamon rolls and world-class scones, the Hi Spot has something for everyone. On warm days, the neighborhood flocks to the outdoor patio for drinks and conversation. The mid-day menu is served from 11:30am and includes fresh salads, sandwiches and soups. This eatery has been a hub of Madrona in the morning hours for some 30 years, and old-timers and newbies alike continue to relish its food and charm.
