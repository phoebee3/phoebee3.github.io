What Is My Flow?

Each person has a unique experience with their "flow" - a place or activity where you feel in your element and lose track of passing time. One place I find my flow is when I'm out in the Cascades hiking. It's one of the places I feel most at home, where I could never get sick of, where I have some of my most memorable experiences.

One place you can go to find hikes for the bucket list is the Washington Trail Association.
