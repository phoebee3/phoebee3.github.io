My Favorite Thing About School

Although I like the courses at EPS, especially history and astronomy, my favorite thing about school is the after-school sports teams. Sports have always been an important part of my life, and this year I've really enjoyed the more competitive atmosphere that cross country and track have provided.

The top three reasons I like the sports teams the most are:

  • Sports provide an opportunity to measure personal growth
  • Friendly competition and team atmosphere build relationships with people from other grades
  • The opportunity to compete helps me understand how I act in high-pressure situations
  • Being able to work with a team is an essential life skill I will use no matter where I end up

The season is just getting started, but you can view our team's times online at athletic.net.
